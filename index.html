The History of Soccer

What is Soccer?

Soccer, also known as football, is a team sport in which two teams of eleven players compete on a rectagular field, a pitch, to score the most goals in a given amount of time by shooting the ball into the net, which is defended by a goalkeeper.

There are many ways to score, but here are a few important ones:

1. Free kicks

2. Penalty kicks

3. Corner kicks

The History

The origin of the sport is not completely certain, as some speculate it could have originiated in ancient China, Greece, Rome, or even Mesoamerica
